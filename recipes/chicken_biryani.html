Chicken Biryani

This is a easy to cook dish, it's authentic and tastes delicious.
Give it a go, you won't regret it.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thigh Fillets (700g)
  • Basmati Rice (450g)
  • Spices
  • Coriander
  • Fried Onions
  • Yoghurt
  1. Marinate for 24 hrs min
  2. Cook Chicken for 5 mins
  3. Get rice read by par boiling for 4 mins
  4. Turn over chicken and cook covered on medium heat for 10 more mins
  5. add onions and coriander once chicken is cooked
  6. add rice cover and cook until steam is coming out and the go low for 15 mins
